Brian Answers Your Questions

What is astrology?▼
Now that's a big question, love! Astrology is the study of how the movements of the planets and stars connect to life down here on Earth. It's been around for thousands of years. I've got my Ephemeris that tracks where every planet is, every single day. Think of it like a cosmic weather forecast. It doesn't tell you what WILL happen, but it shows you the energies that are flowing.
Can the stars predict my future?▼
I'll be honest with you, love -- the stars don't predict your future. They illuminate it. Think of your birth chart as a map of possibilities. It shows your strengths, your tendencies, the energies you were born with. But YOU choose what to do with them. I've read thousands of charts and I've never seen a bad one. Every chart has gifts, every sign has magic.
What's an Ephemeris?▼
An Ephemeris is THE book as far as I'm concerned -- it lists the exact position of every planet for every day of every year. I carried mine everywhere. Even after I lost my sight, I had most of it memorised. With an Ephemeris and a birth time, I can read anyone's stars. It's my bible, love.
What's Mercury retrograde?▼
Everyone panics about Mercury retrograde but honestly, it's not the end of the world. Mercury APPEARS to move backwards in the sky. Things connected to Mercury -- communication, travel, technology -- can get a bit wobbly. But here's what nobody tells you: it's BRILLIANT for revisiting old ideas, reconnecting with people, and finishing things you started. Don't fear it, love. Work with it.
Can you read my palm?▼
Palmistry! Now there's a proper old art. Your palm has four main lines: the heart line, the head line, the life line, and the fate line. Upload a photo of your palm on the Identify tab and I'll have a look for you, love. I might be blind but I've read enough palms to know them by heart!
Are horoscopes real?▼
Your full birth chart has the Moon, Mercury, Venus, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, and more, all in different signs and houses. That's the REAL reading. But even a Sun sign horoscope can give you a nudge in the right direction. Whether you believe the stars are physically pulling strings or just providing a beautiful framework for self-reflection -- either way, it works. And that's the Spooky Do, kid.
Who was Brian Carter?▼
That's me, love! Brian Carter, known as Zodiac Man. I'm from Lancashire, born a showman. I worked the fairgrounds, lived the life. In the 1960s I went to Germany as a gardener and married my German sweetheart -- My Love Indoors, I call her. I carried my Ephemeris everywhere, read the stars for anyone who'd listen. Later in life I lost my sight, but the stars don't care if you can see them. I used to do zodiac readings live on CB radio. Christian, the lad who built this site, he's like a son to me. I believe in you, kid. Always have, always will.
